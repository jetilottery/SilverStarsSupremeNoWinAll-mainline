Silver Stars Supreme

Shine bright and sparkle with Silver Stars Supreme! Match numbers in this glittering game filled with sparkling precious gems! You might even find the Golden Treasure Chest and win all the prizes shown! Collect Bonus Gems to enter the Bonus game for more chances of truly shimmering wins!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Match Your Numbers to Lucky Numbers and win the corresponding prize.

Reveal a 2x symbol to instantly win double the prize amount.

Reveal a Golden Chest symbol and win all prizes instantly.

Reveal 5 Blue Gemstones in Your Numbers to enter the Bonus game.

In the bonus, find 3 or more Blue Gemstones to win a prize. Reveal 3 Silver X symbols or win the top prize to finish the game.

The game ends when all Lucky Numbers, Your Numbers, and bonus items are revealed.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
